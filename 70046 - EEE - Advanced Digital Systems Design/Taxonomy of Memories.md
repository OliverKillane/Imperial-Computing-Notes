## Taxonomy by Access
### Random Access
#### Single Port
*ROM and RAM*
#### Multiport
Provides multiple ports for access to the same data.
#### Special Purpose
*[[Register File]]*
### Associative
*[[Content Addressable Memory|CAM]]*
### Implicit Addressing
*Serial, [[FIFO Memory|FIFO]]/Queue, LIFO/Stack*
